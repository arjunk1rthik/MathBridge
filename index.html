Why Learn From Me?

An award-winning student mathematician passionate about helping younger students succeed.

Hi, I'm Arjun.

I am passionate about helping students build confidence, develop strong mathematical foundations, and become effective problem solvers. I am the winner of Math Olympiad Division E (2022), earned a perfect score of 1860 out of 1860 on the California CAASPP Mathematics Assessment, completed Integrated Mathematics 3A and 3B before high school, and am currently studying Calculus. As a science fair winner, entrepreneur, founder of Scroll Sense, and mathematics educator, I enjoy helping students understand concepts clearly while building confidence and critical thinking skills. My goal is not only to help students improve their grades, but also to help them genuinely enjoy mathematics and develop the problem-solving abilities that will benefit them for years to come. Why Learn From an Advanced High School Student? As an advanced high school mathematics student, I understand what today's students experience because I recently learned—and mastered—the same concepts myself. Instead of relying on memorization or textbook definitions, I explain mathematics in simple, intuitive ways that make difficult ideas finally click. Because I'm closer in age to my students, lessons feel more like a conversation than a lecture. Many younger students feel more comfortable asking questions, admitting when they're confused, and working through mistakes without feeling intimidated. This supportive environment builds confidence, encourages curiosity, and helps students develop a genuine understanding of mathematics. I also know firsthand which concepts schools often move through too quickly. My lessons slow down where necessary, fill in those missing foundations, and connect ideas together so students understand not just how to solve problems, but why the mathematics works. My goal is to help every student build confidence, strengthen critical thinking skills, and develop a love for problem solving that extends far beyond the classroom.
